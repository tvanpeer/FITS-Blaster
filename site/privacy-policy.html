Privacy Policy

App: FITS Blaster  ·  Developer: Tom van Peer  ·  Last updated: March 2026

FITS Blaster does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your Mac.

What data the app accesses

Your image files
The app reads FITS image files from folders you explicitly open. It never modifies, copies, or transmits your files. File access is governed by macOS's sandbox — the app can only reach files and folders you have granted it access to.

Your preferences
Settings such as key bindings, display options, and window layout are stored locally in your Mac's standard user preferences (macOS UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

Security-scoped bookmarks
To remember which folders you have opened across sessions, the app stores a macOS security-scoped bookmark locally on your device. This bookmark contains only the file-system location of your chosen folder — nothing else.

What data the app does NOT collect

  • No personal information (name, e-mail, location, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent to any server
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data shared with third parties
  • No network connections of any kind

Third-party services

The app uses no third-party SDKs, frameworks, or services. It relies exclusively on Apple's own frameworks (SwiftUI, Metal, Accelerate, Foundation).

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. FITS Blaster does not see or store your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. As the app collects no data, material changes are unlikely.
